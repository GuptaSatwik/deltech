ABOUT US

DelTech Baja is an off-road racing team from Delhi Technological University. We design and build an ATV every year to participate in national and international events. In these events we showcase our vehicle enduring tough off-road terrains. Started off in 2003 at Delhi Technological University,DelTech Baja is the oldest Baja SAE team in India and was one of the first teams from Asia to participate in overseas events (South Africa and USA). We, at the core of DelTech Baja, are committed to engineering excellence and this commitment is shown throughout the design and manufacturing of our vehicle, leading us to become one of the top teams in the series.
